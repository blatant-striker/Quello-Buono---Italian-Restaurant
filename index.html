Our Story

Established in 1995, Quello Buono brings the authentic flavors of Italy to your table. Our recipes have been passed down through generations, ensuring an unforgettable dining experience.

Authentic Italian Experience

At Quello Buono, we believe in the power of tradition. Our chefs, trained in the culinary arts of Naples and Rome, bring you dishes that capture the essence of Italy.

Fresh Ingredients

Sourced daily from local farmers and imported directly from Italy

Handcrafted Pasta

Made in-house daily using traditional techniques

Award-Winning Wines

Carefully selected from Italy's finest vineyards
